Mama's Special Beef Pulao

Description

In Pakistan, Pulao is a popular dish cooked with Basmati rice and meat, almost always in beef but sometimes in chicken or mutton as well. Pulao is a rice dish, cooked in seasoned broth with rice, meat, and an array of spices including: coriander seeds, cumin, cardamom, cloves and others.

Ingredients

  • rice 2 cups
  • Onion 1
  • Boiled Beef (with bones) 500 gm
  • Fried onion ½ cup
  • Tomatoes 4 to 5
  • Spring onion 1 cup
  • Red chili flakes 1 tsp
  • Crushed red chilies to taste
  • Oil as required
  • Salt to taste
  • Garlic 3-4 cloves
  • Green chilies 4
  • Cumin 1 tbsp
  • Bay leaf 1 piece
  • Crushed dry coriander 1 tbsp
  • Crushed fennel seeds 1 tbsp
  • Black pepper corn 1 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole red chilies 3
  • Star anise 2
  • Cloves
  • Ginger garlic paste 1 tbsp
  • Oil as required
  • Beef stock 3 cups

Steps

  1. In a pressure cooker, add onion, garlic, beef, whole spices, red chilies, crushed coriander and crushed fennel seeds.
  2. Mix well and allow it to boil for about 10-15 minutes.
  3. Now separate beef and its water in a pan.
  4. Then in a wok heat oil and fry ginger garlic paste for about 2 minutes.
  5. Now add boiled beef, tomatoes, fried onion, red chili flakes, salt and green chilies. Cook well for about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Then add beef’s stock and rice.
  7. Cover and cook properly for about 15 minutes on a low flame.
  8. Lastly garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
